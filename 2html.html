The Life of Martin Luther King Jr.

The man who shaped a nation

Timeline of events in Dr. King's life:

  • 1929 Born in Atlanta, GA
  • 1944 King delivers his first public speech
  • 1951 Graduated with a Bachelor of Divinity from Crozer Theological Seminary
  • 1955 Oversaw Montgomery bus boycott
  • 1963 Delivered famous "I Have a Dream" speech
  • 1964 Won Nobel Peace Prize for combating racial inequality through non-violent means
  • 1968 Assassinated in Memphis, TN
